REFUND POLICY

Refund policies for programs like the Faculty Development Program or workshops at Tirupati Academy typically vary, but they often include the following general terms:

  • If the Academy cancels the program or event, you will likely receive a full refund of the fees paid.
  • You may be eligible for a full or partial refund if you cancel before 7 days of the program begins. The specific amount refunded can depend on how early you cancel as per the academy policy.
  • There may be a deduction for administrative fees in the event of a refund if the cancellation will not receive before the seven days.
  • The Refund amount will be credited directly to the client's account.
